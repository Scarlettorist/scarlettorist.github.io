This is a story written by Zhang Ai’ling, about how two individuals fall in love and finally get married to others. The heroine has a very frustrating fate and a sad ending. The hero finally married someone he hated before. The story happened in the last century in Shanghai. Hero, named Shen Shiyun came from Nanjing and heroine, named Gu Manzhen, came from An’hui. Shen Shiyun was born in a traditional Chinese Family.
